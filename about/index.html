About

28.12.2018 - Jay M. Patel - Reading time ~1 Minute

Hi! My name is Jay M. Patel and I’ve been working fulltime as freelance software developer and data scientist on data mining, web crawling/scraping, natural language processing (NLP) projects.

I am also bootstraping my startup project Specrom Analytics which uses AI algorithms for marketing, social listening and creating alternative financial datasets.

I wrote an algorithmic (or quantitative) trading program for my first client back in 2007 while still in college, and I have been investing in stocks ever since with particular focus on healthcare and biotech stocks.

I graduated with Bachelors in chemical engineering from UDCT, India and M.S. in computational chemistry from University of Georgia, Athens, GA, USA (2013).

After college, I spent about five years working at US Environmental Protection Agency (US EPA) where I used text mining and NLP algorithms to extract useful insights from thousands of documents which were parts of regulatory filings from companies.

In my free time, I volunteer in Dangs district in India to assist tribal community in building homes, getting clean water and sanitation.
